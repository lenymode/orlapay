The peak-time problem

Peak demand is where revenue disappears.

When fans have to choose, pay, wait and ask where to collect, the queue becomes a sales leak.

Peak pressure monitor
4 risks
Sales are lost before checkout

Fans leave the line, buy less, or postpone when the wait feels uncertain.

Staff stop serving and start fixing problems

Operators waste time reconstructing orders, checking payments and explaining pickup instructions.

Managers see the issue too late

Revenue, queues, pickup status and zone performance are often split across separate tools.

The next event starts from guesses

Without clear data, staffing, pricing and layout decisions are based on perception instead of evidence.

Staff workflow

Staff serve orders instead of reconstructing them.

Every order reaches the staff device with items, quantities, payment status, pickup code and next action already clear.

Structured orders No manual interpretation, no screenshots, no handwritten notes.
Payment status visible Staff can see whether the order is paid online, pending Pay-at-Bar, failed or ready for pickup.
Pickup confirmation Deliver by code, confirm handover and keep the pickup queue updated.
Less training during peak hours The workflow is designed for fast onboarding and high-pressure service windows.
